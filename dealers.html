Authorised Manufacturing Partners

Phobos Deimos Engineering does not operate high-volume production lines. Upon completion of the design, prototyping, and manufacturing readiness phase, we refer our clients to the following contract manufacturers for production. These partners have been qualified through our manufacturing process engineering workflow and are familiar with our documentation standards and quality requirements.

Tri-Cities Composites Inc. Cambridge, ON
Carbon-fibre moulding, wet layup, prepreg. ISO 9001:2015. Capacity: 50–200 units/yr per programme.
Aeroprecision Manufacturing Mississauga, ON
CNC machining, sheet metal, assembly. ITAR-registered. AS9100D certified.
Great Lakes Drone Assembly Windsor, ON
Full-system assembly, wiring harness fabrication, QC testing. Prototype-to-production transition specialist.
Pacific NW Composites Portland, OR
Advanced composites, autoclave curing, inspection. US-based partner for ITAR-restricted programmes.

Interested parties should contact us directly for partner qualification information and capability matrices.
