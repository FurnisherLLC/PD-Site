Reference Prototypes

The following platforms are representative of the heavy-lift quadcopter configurations we have developed for client programmes. Client names and production volumes are confidential under non-disclosure agreements. These reference designs illustrate the range of payload capacities, endurance profiles, and environmental specifications that we have delivered to production readiness. All three platforms below underwent full rotor wake interaction analysis, downloadwash characterisation, and drag profiling using our in-house OpenFOAM workflow before any tooling was cut.

CD-6 Survey

Industrial survey and inspection airframe

Configuration4-rotor quadcopter, fixed-pitch, folding arm
MTOW12 kg
Payload Capacity4 kg (survey sensor bay or optical gimbal)
Endurance (nominal)55 minutes
Airframe MaterialCarbon-fibre/epoxy monocoque
Propulsion4x electric brushless with 14 x 4.8 props
AvionicsPixhawk-based flight controller, RTK GNSS, encrypted telemetry
Production Status2 client production runs active

CD-12 Ag

Agricultural spray and application airframe

MTOW25 kg

Propulsion4x electric brushless with 18 x 6.1 props
Environmental SealingIP54 avionics bay with chemical-resistant coatings
