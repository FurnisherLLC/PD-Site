# Phobos Deimos Engineering

Company website for Phobos Deimos Engineering, a Kitchener-based contract engineering firm specialising in heavy-lift drone prototyping, CFD analysis, and manufacturing readiness.

## Pages

- `index.html` — Home page with news feed, engineering process, featured prototypes
- `about.html` — Company background and facility overview
- `prototypes.html` — Reference platform specifications (CD-6 Survey, CD-12 Ag, CD-18 XC)
- `capabilities.html` — In-house engineering capabilities including CFD, airframe design, composites
- `sectors.html` — Agriculture, industrial inspection, and environmental monitoring
- `dealers.html` — Authorised manufacturing partners
- `contact.html` — Contact information and general inquiry form

## Design

Single shared stylesheet (`style.css`). Fixed-width 962px layout with left sidebar navigation. 2012-era B2B industrial aesthetic with navy gradients and minimal JavaScript.
