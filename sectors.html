Industries Served

Our client base spans agricultural technology, industrial inspection, and environmental monitoring. The same core airframe architecture and engineering methodology applies across all three sectors. The differentiators are payload configuration, sensor integration, and environmental specification rather than fundamental airframe design. Why develop separate platforms for each application when a common core can be adapted to all of them?

Agriculture

Precision agriculture platforms for agricultural technology companies, crop consultants, and large-scale cooperatives. Typical applications include:

  • Variable-rate spray airframes from 12 kg to 25 kg MTOW
  • Multispectral NDVI and NDRE mapping configurations
  • Autonomous flight with RTK GNSS guidance
  • IP54 environmental sealing for chemical exposure

Reference platform: CD-12 Ag.

Industrial Inspection and Asset Management

Survey and inspection airframes for infrastructure assessment and industrial asset management. Typical applications include:

  • Linear infrastructure corridor inspection (power lines and pipelines)
  • Solar farm and wind turbine blade inspection
  • Stockpile volumetric survey and site mapping
  • Confined space and hard-to-reach structural inspection

Reference platform: CD-6 Survey.

Environmental Monitoring

Sensor integration packages for environmental research and monitoring programmes. Typical applications include:

  • Wildlife population survey and habitat mapping
  • Water quality monitoring and shoreline assessment
  • Forestry inventory and canopy health analysis
  • Emissions monitoring and atmospheric sampling

Reference platform: CD-18 XC.
