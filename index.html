Welcome to Phobos Deimos Engineering

Phobos Deimos Engineering provides contract engineering services for heavy-lift unmanned aerial systems. We take your payload requirement and operational profile and deliver a production-ready airframe package. Our clients own their intellectual property and production rights. Why build an aerospace division from scratch when you can leverage a team that has already done it 25 times?

Our service scope covers the full engineering lifecycle from aerodynamic analysis and structural design through composites fabrication, flight testing, and manufacturing documentation. We maintain in-house CFD analysis capability using an OpenFOAM-based workflow with custom rotor boundary conditions developed specifically for heavy-lift multirotor configurations. Rather than outsource your critical analysis work, let us handle it alongside the rest of the airframe development.

In-house CFD capability now available for standalone analysis engagements. Rotor wake interaction analysis, downloadwash characterisation for low-altitude operations, drag profiling for underslung payloads, and thermal management simulation for enclosed avionics bays. Our Engineering Process

We operate as a Stage 2 engineering firm. This means we pick up where concept design ends and carry your project through to the point where a contract manufacturer can take over. The process breaks down into four phases.

1 Requirements + Architecture Payload specs, flight performance targets, environmental constraints. We define the design space together with your team. 2 Design + Analysis Airframe engineering, CFD analysis, structural FEA, flight dynamics modelling. Every programme gets the full analysis suite. 3 Prototype + Test Composite fabrication, assembly, flight testing, iteration. We prove the design works before it goes to production. 4 Manufacturing Setup BOMs, tooling specs, assembly procedures, supplier qualification. Complete handoff package for your production partner.
